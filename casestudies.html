case study 1

Description

Georgia Department of Public Health Vaccination Portal Reimagining the state’s COVID-19 and Monkeypox information portal and vaccine appointment scheduler. Amid recent resurgence of highly communicable diseases, the state is overwhelmed with residents wanting to get vaccinated. This portal would provide information on availability and eligibility for vaccines. Visitors may also find vaccine appointments and schedule appointments. However, due to lack of supply, high demand, and lack of information, this process can have many logistical challenges.

Roles

I assumed the UI Designer role for this project.

Deliverables

  • Site map
  • Personas
  • Low-fidelity mockups

Tools

  • Figma
  • Illustrator
  • Canva
  • Octopus

Problems

Vaccine availability is scarce, mostly in rural communities. Information about vaccinations is difficult to find. The existing website did not provide the required information in a way that is friendly to the user.

Solutions

Redesign the state’s COVID-19 and Monkeypox information portal and vaccine appointment scheduler. Redesign the new vaccination information is presented. Address accessibility for the visually impaired.
